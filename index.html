  • What makes Deluxe different from other website hosting providers?

    Deluxe has been a trusted web hosting provider since 2003 and continues to revolutionize the website creation experience. Over 2 million websites are powered by Deluxe and we continue to provide world class services and products to our global customer base.

  • Does Deluxe provide domain names with web hosting?

    When you purchase a shared web hosting package, a free domain is included with your new account. Deluxe customers will have access to verify if their domain name is available along with various domain name extensions to create their unique website. Among the options are .com, .org, .blog, .net and more.

  • Do I get an email address with my web hosting account?

    When you purchase a Shared Hosting Plan and select the “Plus” package you have access to one Microsoft 365 Email Essentials license that is free for 30 days. After 30 days the Microsoft 365 Email Essentials license will automatically renew monthly at the current renewal price until it’s cancelled. Bluehost also offers a variety of email add-on services you can include with your hosting plan for a monthly fee. You can even create an email address that is directly tied to your domain name. [Yourname]@[yourdomain].com

  • Can I transfer my existing domain name to Deluxe?

    Deluxe makes it easy to transfer an existing domain to your Bluehost account. Within your cPanel you will navigate to the domain management section and follow the on-screen instructions to begin your domain transfer. If you need any assistance, please contact our customer support team and we will be happy to help you.
